Auto Components Manufacturing Unit - Case Study

Leading automotive parts supplier in Nashik needed durable flooring
solution for high-traffic manufacturing & assembly area

The Challenge

This automotive facility operated 24/7 with heavy machinery, forklifts, and chemical spilling risks

  • Extreme wear from heavy machinery movement

  • Chemical & oil resistance requirements

  • Need for anti-slip safety surfaces

  • Minimal downtime during installation

  • Long-term durability & low maintenance

  • Cost-effective solution for 5000+ sq.m area

Our Solution

Heavy-Duty PU Flooring System with Chemical-Resistant Coating

CoatCraft Solutions provided a comprehensive PU flooring system specifically designed for the automotive manufacturing environment. The solution combined flexibility, durability, and excellent chemical resistance properties.

  • Superior Impact Resistance - resilient against heavy machinery loads

  • Chemical & Oil Resistant - protects against industrial spillages

  • Anti-Slip Texture - enhanced safety for employees in wet areas

  • Seamless Finish - no joints for bacteria growth or dirt accumulation

Project Results & Business Value

The manufacturing facility achieved significant improvements in
safety, durability, and operational efficiency

Anti-slip PU flooring reduced workplace accidents by 40%, protecting employees from slips and falls in high-risk areas

Lower Maintenance Costs

Seamless, easy-to-clean flooring reduced cleaning time by 50% and maintenance costs by 35% annually

10+ Years Warranty

Durable PU system withstood heavy machinery loads with minimal wear, backed by comprehensive warranty coverage
